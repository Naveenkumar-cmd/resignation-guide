The Founder

Meet Naveen Kumar

Naveen Kumar is the Founder and CEO of Resignation Guide. With 4 years of hands-on experience spanning Sales and IT Consulting, Naveen has worked closely with hundreds of professionals navigating complex career crossroads — from mid-career pivots to high-stakes resignation decisions.

His journey gave him a front-row seat to one of the most painful professional experiences: watching talented people leave jobs impulsively out of frustration, only to land somewhere worse — or staying too long in toxic environments because they lacked the framework to evaluate their options clearly.

Driven by a deep passion for AI, SaaS, and emerging technologies, Naveen set out to build a platform that combines structured self-assessment with AI-powered analysis and human counselor expertise — making professional-grade career guidance accessible to every working professional, not just those who can afford executive coaching.

Naveen's Mission

Passionate about helping working professionals make better career decisions — with data, empathy, and a clear roadmap. He believes every resignation (or the decision to stay) should be made from a position of clarity, not desperation.

Our Story

Started with one conversation

In 2024, Naveen watched a close colleague resign abruptly after a bad appraisal cycle — only to realise six months later they had quit at the worst possible time, forfeiting unvested equity and missing a promotion that was already approved.

That moment sparked a question: what if there was a structured, honest, personalised way to evaluate the resign-or-stay decision before making it?

Resignation Guide was built to answer that question — combining Naveen's experience in Sales, IT Consulting, and his passion for AI and SaaS into a platform that treats professionals as the complex, multi-dimensional humans they are.
